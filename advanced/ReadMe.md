🚧 **One day to practice an IoT topic** 🚧   
⚠️ Before attending, you should have completed a "Hello World" workshop or have learned the basics of IoT and Godot on your own. ⚠️   

---

There are around **30 to 80** main components used in IoT projects when you start working in the industry.   
To cover them all would take **1 to 4 months**, depending on the participants' skill level.    
That won't happen. 😋   

So, I'm going to create and store a series of small workshops of one day that simulate one component at a time using Godot.    
If you want to learn a specific component, feel free to explore and complete the module you need.
